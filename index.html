About Me


Samantha Rieff is a high school English teacher based in Plano, Texas, who has a deep passion for both education and technology. Having grown up in the digital age, Samantha's interest in coding began at an early age, when she started creating simple HTML websites in middle school. Over time, her interest in technology continued to grow and evolve, leading her to pursue more advanced coding skills and explore new areas of interest, including software development and cybersecurity.

In addition to her work as an educator, Samantha is an avid reader and enjoys spending her free time indulging in her favorite hobbies, including knitting, mystery novels, rock climbing, and exploring her love of all things Star Wars and Disney. She is also a big fan of live music and loves attending concerts whenever she can.

Currently, Samantha is enrolled in the January 2023 Women's Cohort Professional Certification Bootcamp through MIT, where she is deepening her knowledge of software development and gaining valuable new skills that will help her advance her career in education and beyond. With her passion for both teaching and technology, Samantha is poised to make a significant impact in both fields in the years to come.
